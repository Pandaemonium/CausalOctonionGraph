## 1. Executive Summary

This search focused on arXiv papers that can inform a COG-style program where:
- spacetime is emergent from causal structure,
- algebraic (octonionic/division algebra) structure is primary,
- update rules should be deterministic and non-perturbative,
- Standard Model structure should emerge with minimal ad hoc inputs.

Main synthesis:
1. **The strongest bridge paper is `2403.00360`**: it explicitly frames CFS and octonionic programs as complementary (vacuum symmetries from octonions, dynamics/spacetime from causal action).
2. **CFS has a substantial formal backbone** (action principle, operator-based spacetime, discrete minimizers, continuum connection).
3. **Division-algebra papers give explicit particle/gauge representation machinery** (ladders, minimal ideals, triality, generation structure), but are often weaker on dynamics.
4. **The main integration gap for COG remains dynamical closure**: an explicit deterministic update law that reproduces the algebraic representation structure without introducing free parameters.

## 3. Tier-1 Papers (Highest Relevance)

### A. Direct CFS-Octonion Bridge

1. **[arXiv:2403.00360](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.00360)**  
   *Causal Fermion Systems and Octonions* (Finster, Gresnigt, Isidro, Marciano, Paganini, Singh, 2024/2024 rev.)  
   Why it matters for COG: establishes the exact conceptual bridge your project is pursuing; explicitly states octonions for vacuum symmetry and CFS for spacetime/dynamics.

### B. CFS Core Theory (for dynamics + causal structure)

2. **[arXiv:1102.2585](https://arxiv.org/abs/1102.2585)**  
   *Causal Fermion Systems: A Quantum Space-Time Emerging from an Action Principle* (Finster, Grotz, Schiefeneder)  
   Relevance: foundational CFS definition and emergence claims (topology, causal structure, spin connection, curvature).

3. **[arXiv:1812.00238](https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.00238)**  
   *Causal Fermion Systems: Discrete Space-Times, Causation and Finite Propagation Speed* (Finster)  
   Relevance: explicitly emphasizes discrete minimizers and finite propagation behavior at macroscales.

4. **[arXiv:1909.09229](https://arxiv.org/abs/1909.09229)**  
   *On the Mathematical Foundations of Causal Fermion Systems in Minkowski Space* (Oppio)  
   Relevance: detailed mathematical treatment of local correlation operator construction and regularity properties.

5. **[arXiv:1409.2568](https://arxiv.org/abs/1409.2568)**  
   *The Continuum Limit of a Fermion System Involving Leptons and Quarks: Strong, Electroweak and Gravitational Interactions* (Finster)  
   Relevance: one of the clearest CFS-to-effective-SM+gravity bridge documents.

### C. Octonionic/Division-Algebra Particle Structure

6. **[arXiv:1603.04078](https://arxiv.org/abs/1603.04078)**  
   *Charge quantization from a number operator* (Furey)  
   Relevance: compact algebraic mechanism for charge quantization and unbroken `SU(3)_c × U(1)_em`.

7. **[arXiv:1910.08395](https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.08395)**  
   *Three generations, two unbroken gauge symmetries, and one eight-dimensional algebra* (Furey)  
   Relevance: direct three-generation octonionic representation construction, close to COG generation goals.

8. **[arXiv:2209.13016](https://arxiv.org/abs/2209.13016)**  
   *One generation of standard model Weyl representations as a single copy of R⊗C⊗H⊗O* (Furey, Hughes)  
   Relevance: addresses fermion doubling concerns and gives a concrete representation package.

### D. Triality + Exceptional Jordan Anchors

9. **[arXiv:2006.16265](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.16265)**  
   *The Standard Model, The Exceptional Jordan Algebra, and Triality* (Boyle)  
   Relevance: explicit triality/generation interpretation and geometric packaging using complex octonionic geometry.

10. **[arXiv:1806.09450](https://arxiv.org/abs/1806.09450)**  
    *Deducing the symmetry of the standard model from the automorphism and structure groups of the exceptional Jordan algebra* (Todorov, Dubois-Violette)  
    Relevance: group-theoretic route to SM symmetry via exceptional Jordan structures.

11. **[arXiv:1911.13124](https://arxiv.org/abs/1911.13124)**  
    *Exceptional quantum algebra for the standard model of particle physics* (Todorov)  
    Relevance: additional exceptional-Jordan/triality perspective for generation and symmetry structure.

## 5. What This Means for COG (Actionable)

### 5.1 Strongest Immediate Research Direction
Use `2403.00360` as the top-level architecture hypothesis:
- **Octonionic layer** defines vacuum algebra and representation structure.
- **CFS-like causal action layer** provides dynamical update principle on causal structure.

For COG, this maps cleanly to:
- locked algebraic conventions and state encoding (already in place),
- deterministic update law + causal optimization criterion (currently partial),
- emergent geometry as large-graph limit (still open).

### 5.2 Concrete Next Formalization Targets
1. Define a **discrete causal action analog** in graph terms (integer-valued or rational objective over local operator configurations).
2. Prove that `step` is both:
   - deterministic under RFC-002 schedule rules, and
   - descent-preserving (or stationary-point preserving) for the chosen action.
3. Add a CFS-inspired claim file set:
   - `claims/causal_action_discrete.yml`
   - `claims/minimizer_discreteness.yml`
   - `claims/vacuum_symmetry_from_octonions.yml`
   - Status (2026-02-24): created as `status: stub` roadmap claims.

### 5.3 Risk Notes for COG
- Division-algebra papers are often rich on representation structure but under-specified dynamically.
- CFS papers are rich on dynamics and geometry but not natively in COG’s strict finite-combinatorial simulation form.
- The integration challenge is therefore technical, not conceptual: define a finite update/action pair that preserves COG’s determinism and no-free-parameter constraints.
