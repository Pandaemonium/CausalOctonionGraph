## Paper 1: Octonion Internal Space Algebra for the Standard Model

- **Authors:** Ivan Todorov
- **arXiv:** [2206.06912v3](https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.06912v3) (hep-th, hep-ph, math-ph)
- **Published:** 2022-06-14

### Key claims
1. The SM internal space algebra is built from Clifford algebras generated by **left multiplication by octonions** ($C\ell_{10}$).
2. A distinguished complex structure implements the splitting $\mathbb{O} = \mathbb{C} \oplus \mathbb{C}^3$, encoding **lepton-quark symmetry**.
3. The projector $\mathcal{P} = \frac{1}{2}(1 - i\omega_6)$, built from the $C\ell_6$ volume form, isolates a **16-dimensional particle subspace** (annihilating antiparticles).
4. The SM gauge group $SU(3)_c \times SU(2)_L \times U(1)_Y$ emerges as the subgroup of the Pati-Salam group $G_{\text{PS}} = Spin(4) \times Spin(6) / \mathbb{Z}_2$ that **preserves the sterile neutrino** (identified with the Fock vacuum).
5. The Higgs field arises as the scalar part of a **superconnection** (odd element of $C\ell_4^1$).
6. Colour confinement is algebraically guaranteed: the projection onto $C\ell_6^0$ ensures **colour symmetry remains unbroken**.

- **Authors:** N. Furey, M. J. Hughes
- **arXiv:** [2210.10126v1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.10126v1) (hep-ph, hep-th)
- **Published:** 2022-10-18

### Key claims

4. Upon breaking $SU(2)_R$, the quaternionic-triality Higgs reduces exactly to the **familiar SM Higgs doublet**.
